Meet Palvi Gulati

E-RYT 500 · 8+ years · Ashtanga, Prenatal & Postnatal specialist

I am Palvi Gulati, a certified Ashtanga, prenatal, and postnatal yoga teacher based in Jammu. I have been running my own yoga studio for the past eight years, helping people improve their physical health, mental well-being, and overall lifestyle through yoga. I specialize in supporting women during pregnancy and postnatal recovery with safe and effective practices, while also guiding students of all levels to build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness in their daily lives. 🧘‍♀️🌿

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first class?

Just bring comfortable clothing, a yoga mat if you have one, and a water bottle. We have mats available if needed.

Are online classes live or recorded?

All online classes are live via Zoom, so you can interact and get personalized guidance. Recordings are available for members.

Do I need prior yoga experience?

Not at all! My classes welcome all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. I offer modifications for every pose.

Where are you located in Trikuta Nagar?

My studio is near the main market, above the post office. Exact address will be shared upon booking your first session.
